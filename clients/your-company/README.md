# Your Company (blank template)

This folder is for **your own company or agency** - the business you run. Its brand voice, positioning, ideal customer, and offers live here, separate from your personal brand and from the clients you serve.

If you run an agency, this is your agency's own brand. The companies you do work FOR get their own folders (see `example-client`).

## How to set it up

```bash
cd clients/your-company
claude
```

Claude sees the empty `brand_context/` and runs `/start-here` to build your company's brand foundation. Or fill the templates in by hand.

Rename the folder to your company's slug (lowercase, hyphens) if you like. If you do not run a separate company, delete this folder.
