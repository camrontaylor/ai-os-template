## How it works

Three steps. You do almost none of them.

**1. Send us what you've got.**
Bank logins, receipts, that half-finished accounting file, the shoebox. Whatever state it's in. Messy is the normal starting point.

**2. We get you caught up.**
A real, named bookkeeper cleans up your past months, sorts every transaction, and gets your books current. You don't learn any software. You don't categorize anything.

**3. You get a summary by the 10th, every month.**
Two lines a human can read, then the numbers behind them. You'll always know exactly where you stand. That's the whole point.

## FAQ

**My books are a total mess. Is that a problem?**
No. It's the normal starting point. Catch-up is literally the first thing we do. You will never be turned away for being behind.

**Do I have to learn accounting software?**
No. You send us what you have and we do the rest. You read a short summary each month. That's your whole job.

**Is this just software with a chatbot?**
No. A real, named human reads your books and does the work. You can email them. They know your business.

**Do you do my taxes?**
We keep your books clean and current so tax season is easy, and we work hand in hand with your CPA. Filing your return is a separate job that your CPA handles. We keep that line clear on purpose so you always know who does what.

**What if I'm not happy?**
Cancel any time, no contract. You keep your books.
