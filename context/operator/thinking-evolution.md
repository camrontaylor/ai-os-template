# Thinking Evolution (template)

Optional. A log of settled decisions, so you and the assistant do not re-litigate
choices that are already made. When something is decided, record it here with the
date and the reasoning. This is your anti-churn reference.

## How to use this

- Add an entry when you settle a real decision you do not want to revisit.
- Keep each entry short: the decision, the date, and why.
- If you genuinely change your mind later, add a new dated entry rather than
  deleting the old one.

## Settled decisions

- [YYYY-MM-DD]: [decision] - [one line on why].
